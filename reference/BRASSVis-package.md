# BRASSVis: Visualization of Structural Variants from BRASS

Visualize gene fusions and structural variants detected by BRASS
(BReakpoint AnalySiS) from whole-genome sequencing data. Reads BEDPE
files and produces publication-ready PDF reports showing breakpoints,
exon structure, ideograms, circos plots, and protein domain retention.
Based on the Arriba visualization approach adapted for WGS structural
variant data.
